Requirements

Check your phone before installing.

Compatibility depends on Android, Nothing OS package names, and whether the device exposes the Essential Key through hardware key events.

Required

  • Android 11 or later for embedded Wireless Debugging setup.
  • A Nothing or CMF device that includes the Essential Space handler packages.
  • Developer Options and temporary Wireless Debugging access during setup.
  • Permission to enable the app's hardware-button Accessibility service.

Current compatibility status

There is not yet a formally verified per-model compatibility matrix. Treat support as experimental until your exact device and Nothing OS version have been confirmed.

Known limitations

  • Nothing OS updates may change package names or hardware-key delivery.
  • Another Accessibility service requesting hardware key events may conflict.
  • Flashlight access can fail while the camera is using the flash.
  • Launching apps or URLs can be affected by Android background activity restrictions.
  • Silent-mode behavior may require Notification Policy access.

Report confirmed device results through GitHub Issues, including device model, Android version, Nothing OS version, and the setup step that succeeded or failed.

Compatibility claims are intentionally conservative.
